Our rest Enjoying the beach

Many beautfull beaches located in Rwanda are the good places where we can pass
our holldays, resting after our job; felling the blaze from not salty water of our lakes.

By Furebo Didace on Wednesday August the 12th 2020

In Rwanda country also called the country of one thousand hills we find many beautifull areas such as montains ,lakes, hills Valleys, forests ,parks,and other historical areas where most of peoples are curries of and need to visit including the foreigners. But today i talk about the rwandan lakes with their beautifull beaches where we reach and we fell comfortable, resting , enjoying ourselves particulary in weekends and holydays.
